Your Settings can be ERASED on accident!

Everything you configure - General Presets, Price Models and Hourly Labor Rates - is stored only in this browser‚Äôs localStorage. This is intentional, as the app does not rely on a server to run. Clearing cookies / site-data, or downloading a fresh copy of the app from the github repo will remove all your settings. We want you to be able to enjoy future updates, without having to manually re-apply each and every setting, everytime you download a new version.

Keeping a backup is easy:

  • In General Presets click Export General Presets to download a .txt file containing plain-text JSON document. Restore these settings by clicking Import Presets and pasting the file's contents in the provided window.
  • Similarily, in the Price Models tab, each row has a button labeled Export Price Model . Restore a price model to the app by clicking Import Price Model and pasting the file's contents in the provided window.
  • Hourly Labor Rates aren‚Äôt covered by the export buttons. If they‚Äôre lost you‚Äôll need to recreate them via Manage Presets → Hourly Labor Rates → Create New Hourly Labor Rate .

Store these downloaded files somewhere safe and accessible, and you‚Äôll always be only a few copy-paste commands away from restoring your settings.
